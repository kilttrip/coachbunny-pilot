# Public safety notes (concept only)

This page is a teaser for a pilot concept in research. It is not travel advice, and it does not verify services, operators, schedules, fares, or availability.

## What travellers should do
- Verify routes, schedules, conditions, and requirements using official sources and the relevant operators.
- Book directly with the relevant operators if you choose to travel.
- Keep contingency time between legs; avoid planning tight connections.
- Check passport/ID validity and any entry/transit requirements applicable to your trip.
- Consider travel insurance and accessible travel needs early.
- Share your itinerary with a trusted contact when traveling late or alone.

## Situational awareness reminders
- Late-night transit can change quickly; have a backup plan and safe meeting points.
- Keep valuables secured; stay alert in crowded stations and ports.
- Know your local emergency number and the location of staffed help points.

## Service boundary reminder
CoachBunny does not operate transport, ferries, taxis, venues, marinas, restaurants, clubs, jobs, housing, events, or tours. CoachBunny does not sell tickets. This Hop is currently a pilot concept in research. Travellers must verify and book directly with relevant operators and official sources.
